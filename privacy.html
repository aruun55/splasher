When you use Splasher, we collect the following types of data:

We collect personal details you provide when creating an account, including but not limited to:

- **Full Name**

- **Email Address**

- **Phone Number**(optional)

- **Date of Birth**

- **Profile Picture** (optional)

 

### 1.2 Location Data

Splasher is a location-based game that requires real-time **GPS tracking** for gameplay features.

- We collect **precise location data** when the app is active and running in the background (if permitted).

- Your location is used to facilitate in-game interactions and may be visible to other players as part of game mechanics.

- You can **disable location tracking** via your device settings, but this will affect gameplay.

 

### 1.3 Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect information about your device and how you interact with the App, including:

- **Device Type & Model**

- **Operating System & Version**

- **IP Address**

- **App Usage Statistics (game actions, session duration, clicks, etc.)**

- **Crash Reports & Performance Metrics**

 

### 1.4 Payment Information

If you make in-app purchases, we collect:

- **Transaction Details (Amount, Date, Payment Method)**

- Payment processing is securely handled by third-party providers in compliance with **RBI (Reserve Bank of India) guidelines**.

 

## 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

 

We use the information collected to:

 

1. Provide and improve game features and user experience.

2. Enable **location-based gameplay mechanics**.

3. Ensure **user safety** and prevent fraudulent activities.

4. Process payments and provide **customer support**.

5. Analyze app performance and **detect security threats**.

6. Comply with **legal obligations and law enforcement requests**.

 

We do **not** use your personal data for purposes unrelated to Splasher without your explicit consent.

 

## 3. LOCATION TRACKING POLICY

 

- Splasher relies on **real-time GPS tracking** for gameplay.

- **Why we need it**: The game involves real-world movement and interactions.

- **Who can see your location**: Only players within the game who have a reason to see your location (e.g., assigned hunters or targets).

- **How long we store it**: Location data is not stored permanently; it is only used during active gameplay sessions.

- **Disabling Location**: You can turn off location tracking via **device settings**, but it may make the game unplayable.
